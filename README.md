# superdungeon

=== EVERETT'S SUPERDUNGEON - ADVENTURER'S GUIDE ===

🎮 WELCOME TO THE SUPERDUNGEON! 🎮

══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
📋 PARTY SELECTION - CHOOSE YOUR HEROES
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

🗡️ BARBARIAN - The Berserker
Pure physical powerhouse. Rage doubles Attack damage at target, but Barbarian will take double damage for the round.

🔮 SORCERESS - The Destroyer
Master of Spells. Immense wide-area destructive output, but extremely fragile. Learns the exclusive and hard-hitting Omni spell.

⛪ BISHOP - The Divine
Master of Prayers. Immense healing and buff output, but extremely fragile. Learns the exclusive Miracle prayer, which completely restores an ally's HP and grants Empower3, Lifelink, and Fury all at once!

⚔️ VALKYRIE - The Commander
Balanced fighter with access to Prayers. Can use the Rally command to restore a small amount of HP and MP to the entire party.

🥷 NINJA - The Assassin
Balanced fighter with access to Spells. Can use the Spellblade command to deal significant single-target damage.

🌟 SHAMAN - The Elementalist
Balanced magic-user that can cast Spells and Prayers with equal proficiency.

🥋 MONK - The Martial Artist
Stalwart martial-artist with access to Arts as Monk increases in level. Can manipulate turn order with Rapid (two weak attacks that always go first) and Focus (heavy-hitting blow that's always last), attack multiple foes at once (Kick and Rapid), and even heal itself (Zen).

🌿 SYLVAN - The Shapeshifter
Dynamic shapeshifter. Shift command transforms the Sylvan into an animal for a limited time. Be careful, you cannot control the Sylvan while they are feral! Bear is a bulky juggernaut; Unicorn boosts healing of your allies and revives the party; Cobra hits hard and always goes first.



══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
⚔️ COMBAT SYSTEM
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

📊 STATS THAT MATTER:
• HP: Your life force - reach 0 and you're KO'd!
• MP: Magic Points - needed for Spells and Prayers
• STR: Physical attack power and weapon damage
• DEF: Physical defense against enemy attacks  
• INT: Magic attack power for Spells
• MND: Magic defense against enemy Spells

🎯 SPELL MAGIC (Offensive):
• Fire/Frost/Shock/Hydro: Elemental damage + stat debuffs
• Poison: Deals 5% of target's maximum health at the end of the round for 5 turns.
• Higher levels = more damage, longer debuffs
• Each element weakens a different stat!
• Omni hits for massive damage

🙏 PRAYER MAGIC (Support):
• Heal: Restore HP (scales with level + caster's stats)
• Empower:  Multiply physical damage (1.5x/3x/5x for levels 1/2/3!) for 4 rounds*
• Fury: Double attack on every turn for 4 rounds*
• Lifelink: Heal for 50% of damage dealt for 4 rounds *
• Restore: Remove poison effects
• Revive: Bring back fallen allies with 25% HP
• Miracle: Full heal + all buffs (Empower3, Lifelink, Fury) + works on KO'd allies!
*Winning a round extends the duration of your buffs!

🥋 MONK ARTS (Physical Abilities - 0 MP Cost!):
• Rapid: Strike 2 random enemies with 50% strength (4 with Fury!) - always acts first!
• Zen: Self-heal for HP
• Focus: Devastating single-target strike - always acts last!
• Kick: Hit all enemies at once (no Fury bonus)
• Arts are ALWAYS available - no MP required!
• Unless otherwise noted; Works with Empower, Fury, and Lifelink buffs!


══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
💥 SPECIAL ATTACKS - SPECIAL COMMAND SYSTEM
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Every character has a powerful SPECIAL ability that charges during combat!

⚡ CHARGING YOUR LIMIT GAUGE:
• Taking damage fills your gauge (more damage = faster charging!)
• Defeating enemies grants gauge to your whole party
• Watch the bar below each character's sprite
• Gauge turns from gold to pink when fully charged!

🌟 USING YOUR SPECIAL:
• At 100% gauge, "Attack" becomes "✨ Special ✨" (rainbow text!)
• Specials cost 0 MP - always usable when ready!
• Specials have HIGHEST priority - they always go first!
• Using a Special depletes your gauge back to 0%

⚠️ GAUGE RESETS:
• Getting KO'd resets your gauge to 0%
• Entering the Tavern resets all gauges
• Gauge persists between waves within a dungeon

💀 CLASS SPECIALS:

🗡️ BARBARIAN - Bloodfury
   Unleash 11 rapid strikes on random enemies, healing for all damage dealt!
   
⚔️ VALKYRIE - Battle Cry
   Rally your allies! Grants Empower3 + Fury to the ENTIRE party and heals everyone!
   
🥷 NINJA - Night Slash
   Strike ALL enemies with a devastating shadow attack and poison them all!
   
🔮 SORCERESS - Meteor Storm
   Rain destruction from above! Multiple magic hits on random enemies with stat debuffs!
   
🌟 SHAMAN - Elemental Fury
   Channel all four elements in sequence: Hydro, Fire, Frost, then Shock - hitting all enemies!
   
⛪ BISHOP - Angelic Chorus
   Divine intervention! Fully restore HP and MP for the entire party AND revive all fallen allies!
   
🥋 MONK - Nirvana Fist
   Achieve enlightenment! Full self-heal, gain all buffs (Empower3/Fury/Lifelink), then deliver a massive strike!

💡 SPECIAL TIPS:
• Barbarians charge faster when Raging (taking 2x damage = 2x gauge!)
• Glass cannon classes (Sorceress, Bishop) charge quickly due to low HP
• Tanks (Monk, Valkyrie) charge slower but their Specials are worth the wait
• Empower and Lifelink buffs enhance damage-dealing Specials!
• Save Specials for tough waves or boss fights for maximum impact!

🌍 WORLD PROGRESSION  
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Every 15 waves = New Dungeon with tougher enemies!
1. Overworld (Waves 1-15): Rats, Goblins, Kobolds
2. Desert (Waves 16-30): Sand Worms, Thieves, Mummies
3. Catacombs (Waves 31-45): Skeletons, Oozes, Banshees
4. Jungle (Waves 46-60): Raptors, Megasaurus, Nessiedon
5. Badlands (Waves 61-75): Doomhounds, Dracoguards, Elite Kobolds
6. ??? (Waves 76-90): Mi-go, Shoggoths, Elder Things
7. Arena (Waves 91+): Post-game endless waves mode unlocked after defeating the final boss

🐉 BOSS FIGHTS (Every 15th Wave):
Dragons with devastating area attacks!
• Dragon Breath: Hits all party members for 25% max HP
• Bosses act twice per turn
• Final Boss: The King in Yellow awaits at Wave 90!

🏆 VICTORY & THE ARENA (Waves 91+):
• Defeat the King in Yellow to win the game!
• Choose to continue into the endless Arena mode
• Arena features random enemies from ALL worlds
• Enemies hit harder but have less defense - true glass cannons!
• How far can you go?

🏪 TAVERN (After Each Dungeon):
• Full party restoration (HP, MP, revive fallen)
• Change character classes - experiment freely!
• Stats recalculate based on your current level
• Character level determined by # of rounds a character has won (fainted party members do not level up!)

🎯 STRATEGY TIPS
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

✨ PARTY COMPOSITION
• Barbarian and hybrid classes start stronger. 
• Sorceress and Bishop excel at late game
• Always have 1 Barbarian, 1 spell user, 1 prayer user.


🔥 COMBAT TACTICS:
• Buff before boss fights during waves 13 & 14; Empower + Fury = destruction!
• Use elemental spells for debuffs, not just damage
• Poison works great on high-HP enemies
• Don't forget - Rage makes Barbarians glass cannons!
• Save Specials for emergencies or boss fights!

🌟 POWER PROGRESSION:
• Characters unlock new abilities every few levels
• Higher-level spells have stronger debuff effects
• Late-game abilities can change everything
