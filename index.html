      📖 Game Guide

      ⚔ Basic Controls ▼
      COMBAT BASICS
      • Tap a command (Attack, Spell, etc.) to select it
      • Tap an enemy to target them
      • Tap an ally for healing/buffs
      • Actions execute in speed order each round
      • Swipe left/right on class select to cycle classes
      👤 Character Classes ▼
      BARBARIAN - The Berserker
      Pure physical powerhouse. Uses Rage for massive damage but takes 2x damage while active. Charges Special fastest due to damage taken.

      VALKYRIE - The Commander
      Balanced fighter with strong physical attacks. Battle Cry Special buffs entire party with Empower3 + Fury.

      NINJA - The Assassin
      Fast and deadly. Mixes physical attacks with offensive magic (Fire, Poison). Night Slash poisons all enemies.

      SORCERESS - The Destroyer
      Pure offensive magic. Fire spells + Slow debuff. Glass cannon with low HP but devastating Meteor Storm Special.

      SHAMAN - The Elementalist
      Master of elements. Hydro (↓MND), Fire (↓STR), Frost (↓DEF), Shock (↓INT). Elemental Fury casts all four.

      BISHOP - The Divine
      Ultimate healer. Powerful healing prayers + Revive. Angelic Chorus Special fully heals and revives entire party.

      MONK - The Martial Artist
      Tactical fighter using Arts (0 MP cost). Highest DEF in game. Focus hits hard but goes last. Dodge avoids all damage.

      SYLVAN - The Shapeshifter
      Transforms into animal forms (Bear, Unicorn, Cobra) for 3 turns each. Phoenix Form Special grants immortality + devastation.
      ⚡ Combat Mechanics ▼
      TURN ORDER:
      1. Specials (100% Limit Gauge)
      2. Always First (Monk: Rapid, Dodge, Taunt)
      3. Boss Abilities (Dragon Breath)
      4. Normal Actions (by speed)
      5. Always Last (Monk: Focus)

      BUFFS:
      • Empower1/2/3: 2x/3.5x/5x damage
      • Fury: Attack twice per turn
      • Lifelink: Heal 50% of damage dealt

      DEBUFFS:
      • Poison: 5% max HP per turn
      • Slow: Reduced speed
      • Stat Downs: -15% to STR/DEF/INT/MND

      ELEMENTS:
      • Hydro → ↓MND (more magic damage)
      • Fire → ↓STR (less physical damage)
      • Frost → ↓DEF (take more physical)
      • Shock → ↓INT (less magic damage)
      • Darkness → ↓ALL stats
      💥 Limit Break System ▼
      CHARGING:
      • Take damage: (Damage/MaxHP) × 50% gauge
      • Defeat enemy: +2% (killer) / +1% (allies)

      RESETS:
      • Using Special → 0%
      • Character KO → 0%
      • Returning to Tavern → 0%

      Each class has a unique Special ability at 100% gauge.
